Safety

While moose are adorable, there are many safety guidelines that apply to moose owners.

  1. Don't leave it cooped up in one place for too long.
  2. Give it food and water 4 times a day, and if it starts snorting, give it a snack.
  3. Steer clear of the antlers. They may not look sharp, but you wouldn't want to get poked!
  4. Make sure to replace its hoof-guard once a month. Otherwise, the hooves could tear up carpets and plants
  5. Give it a comfortable amount of space. The kennel provided with the Gold and Platinum should suffice for several hours and sleep, but be sure to take it out every once in a while.
  6. The most important thing with owning a moose is that you love it. Moose are gentle, and if you love it, it will love you.

About MooseCorp

MooseCorp was founded in 219 by Naman Mishra with the purpose of showing people the love between mankind and moosekind.

"If you have not ever known the love between a human and moose, your life has not yet been completed." -William Shakespeare

Although there are many other corporations that offer to help you adopt a certain animal, we are the only one founded on love. There are lots of things we do to ensure that your moose is at its kindest, calmest, and most lovable form. Some examples are-

  1. Always treating our moose with the most love and respect we can
  2. Following all of our safety guidelines
  3. Raising them in large, wide enclosures that simulate their natural habitat
  4. Never separating families
  5. Vaccinating them to make sure they are always healthy

We hope that, passing this moose onto you, we are giving it a life equal, if not better, than the life it has lived so far.
